When lifes got you down Welcome to HAPPY DAY, we are a social media website that prides on the blocking of negative energy. Our goal is to filter out specific words to lower cyber bullying through the internet to prevent social shaming. In today's society social shaming can lead to things such as suicide or self-harm. Social media websites such as twitter, facebook, and instagram promote such cyber bullying and social shaming. Sometimes leading to depression or other serious acts against oneself. Social shaming is something that exists in today's society that can be prevented by bring only happiness, hence our application HAPPY DAY. Happy Day is an application designed to filter out certain words and context based off of its overall happiness. This application's goal is to prevent cyber bullying across social media platforms. Social shaming and things of the such should not be tolerated in today's society. Individuals should be able to use social media application without the worry of being judged by their peers or any others. Our overall goal with HAPPY DAY is to maximize happiness similar to a utilitarian standpoint. Any words seen as 'unhappy' in our database will return a null value not allowing that content to be posted as a result.
